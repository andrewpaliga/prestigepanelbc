SIPs from Prestige Panel™

Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs) from Prestige Panel™ are a revolutionary technology that will save you time, energy, money and labour.

They contains the interior wall board, insulation, vapour barrier, structural support and exterior sheeting all in one panel assembly.

Rapidly snap the panels together to frame and insulate your building envelope in days, not weeks.

Safer, stronger, more comfortable
